# The Viva — Questions You Must Answer

If you have been writing your learning log and documenting decisions, these questions will not surprise you. If you skipped those steps, you will struggle.

**Authentication:**
- What is the difference between authentication and authorization?
- Why do you hash passwords? What algorithm does EduFlow use?
- Explain the two-token pattern. Why is the refresh token stored hashed?
- What is an IDOR vulnerability? Show me where EduFlow prevents one.

**Database:**
- Why did you choose MongoDB over PostgreSQL for an LMS?
- What is a Mongoose pre-save hook used for in EduFlow?
- What is the N+1 query problem? How does EduFlow's catalogue prevent it?
- What does select: false on passwordHash do?

**Payments:**
- How does Stripe's webhook system work? Why might a webhook arrive twice?
- What is idempotency and why does the enrollment service need it?
- What is the price snapshot problem and how is it solved?

**Scale:**
- What is the cache-aside pattern?
- Why must the catalogue cache be invalidated when a course is published?
- What is cursor pagination and why is it better than offset at scale?

Write your answers in learning-log/99-viva-prep.md and commit them.
