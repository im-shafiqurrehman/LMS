# Course Completion Check

The course completion check runs after every Mark Complete action. It should be efficient — not a blocking N+1 query.

The current implementation: two countDocuments queries. Both use indexes ({courseId} and {enrollmentId}). At the scale of a typical course (20-50 lessons), these are sub-millisecond operations.

At very large scale (millions of students), you could cache the totalLessons count per course in Redis (it only changes when the instructor adds or removes lessons). But for EduFlow's current stage, two indexed countDocuments queries are fine.

✅ Verify: after marking the last lesson complete, `courseCompleted: true` is returned and a Certificate document exists in MongoDB.
