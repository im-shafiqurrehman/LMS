# Interesting to Read

**1. Stripe's PaymentIntents lifecycle documentation**
Search "Stripe PaymentIntents how it works" — the official guide explains what happens between intent creation and payment confirmation, including 3DS authentication flows.

**2. Database transactions and idempotency**
Search "idempotency keys distributed systems" — there is excellent writing by Brandur Leach (Stripe engineer) on building idempotent APIs. Required reading if you will work on financial systems.

**3. Webhook security**
Search "Stripe webhook signature verification" — understand why Stripe signs webhooks (to prevent fake webhook attacks) and how constructEvent() verifies the signature.
