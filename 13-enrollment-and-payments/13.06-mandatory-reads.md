# Mandatory Reads

Before implementing the payment flow, read:

**1. Stripe PaymentIntents guide**
Search "Stripe PaymentIntents guide". Understand the lifecycle: created → requires_action → processing → succeeded. EduFlow's webhook handler only acts on payment_intent.succeeded.

**2. Stripe webhook events and idempotency**
Search "Stripe webhook idempotency best practices". Understand why Stripe may deliver the same event more than once, and why your handler must be idempotent (same input, same result, no duplicates).

**3. Stripe test card numbers**
Search "Stripe testing test card numbers". The test card 4242 4242 4242 4242 always succeeds. 4000 0000 0000 0002 always declines. You will need both to test the payment flow.

These reads take 30 minutes. They are required — the webhook handler is the most critical piece of this chapter.
