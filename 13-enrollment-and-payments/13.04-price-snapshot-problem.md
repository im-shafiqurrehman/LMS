# The Price Snapshot Problem

An instructor creates a course at $49. One hundred students enroll. Next month, the instructor raises the price to $99.

If Enrollment stores only courseId, and the display logic reads course.price, those 100 students now appear to have paid $99 for their courses. Refund calculations, revenue reports, and student receipts are all wrong.

The fix: store amountPaid on the Enrollment document at enrollment time. It is the price the student actually paid, frozen at the moment of payment. It never changes regardless of what happens to the course price later.

```
enrollment.amountPaid = course.price  // at time of payment
```

This is a price snapshot, not a cache. Do not try to "keep it in sync" — it is intentionally immutable after creation.

This decision was made in the data model (chapter 05). Now you see why it matters.
