# Why Stripe

Stripe is the industry standard for online payments in developer-facing products. Its documentation is exceptional, its sandbox environment is fully functional without real money, and its webhook system is mature.

For EduFlow specifically:
- Stripe's PaymentIntent API handles retries, partial authorizations, and 3DS (Strong Customer Authentication) automatically
- Stripe's webhook system delivers payment confirmations reliably, with retries if your server is temporarily unreachable
- Stripe's test card numbers (4242 4242 4242 4242) make development predictable

The alternative (building your own payment processing) would require PCI DSS compliance — a certification process that costs thousands of dollars and takes months. Stripe handles PCI DSS; you inherit its compliance by not handling raw card data.

You need a Stripe account for this chapter. The free tier works for development — no real money is charged in test mode.
