# Course Completion Indicator

When courseCompleted: true is returned by the mark-complete endpoint, show a completion banner:

```tsx
{showCertificatePrompt && (
  <div style={{ background: '#d4edda', border: '1px solid #c3e6cb', padding: '1rem', borderRadius: 4, marginTop: '1rem' }}>
    <h2>Congratulations! You have completed the course.</h2>
    <p>You can now download your certificate.</p>
    <button onClick={handleIssueCertificate}>Get Certificate</button>
  </div>
)}
```

The certificate issuance button calls POST /api/learn/certificates/:courseId and then offers a download link.
