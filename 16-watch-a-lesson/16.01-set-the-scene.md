# Watch a Lesson — Set the Scene

This chapter builds the lesson player experience in Next.js: a video player, the lesson sidebar for navigation, a progress tracker, a Mark Complete button, a certificate download link, and the Q&A tab. The EnrollmentGuard from chapter 14 wraps the entire player page.

By the end: a student can watch a lesson, mark it complete, see their progress, and (when all lessons are done) download their certificate.
