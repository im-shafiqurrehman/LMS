# Enroll in a Course — Set the Scene

The payment backend is ready. This chapter adds the Stripe Elements checkout form to the Next.js frontend, handles the payment result, and shows the student their enrolled courses on a My Learning page.

By the end:
- /courses/[id] shows an Enroll button for unenrolled students, or a Go to Course button for enrolled students
- /checkout/[courseId] shows the Stripe payment form
- After payment, the student is redirected to /dashboard with their enrollment confirmed
- /dashboard/my-learning shows the student's enrolled courses
